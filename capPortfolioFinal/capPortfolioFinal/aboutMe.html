About Me

Hello! My name is Jaelynn Palma!

Growing up, I’ve always been interested in creating things and understanding how they work. Computer science has helped me express that interest through coding and problem-solving. Aside from my coding, I also enjoy playing basketball and engaging in hands-on activities such as crafting.

This portfolio showcases the work I’ve completed during the Introduction to Computer Science course. It demonstrates how I’ve grown both as a problem-solver and a coder. Throughout the course, I’ve faced challenges, made progress, and developed valuable skills.

When I first started this class, I didn’t know much about coding. At times, I was confused and stuck. However, I eventually learned to understand and break down problems and think logically. With each project, I built more confidence in using programming concepts such as conditionals, loops, variables, and functions. Computer science has taught me how to approach problems with patience and creativity. As I kept practicing, I became more comfortable turning ideas into working projects. Now, I feel more confident in my coding skills and proud of the progress I’ve made throughout the course
